Foreign Trade Policy

One of the most important factors of any prospering economy is what that country does to establish trading relations with other countries. As you read this informational page, you will learn what policies the leaders of Petoria use to handle trade.

Trading Partners

Petoria specializes in the production of cocoa beans. We have established trading relationships with countries in 5 of the 7 continents.

  • The United States
  • The United Kingdom
  • Morocco
  • Spain
  • Sweden
  • China
  • Egypt
  • Isreal
  • And Madagascar

Balance of Trade

Petoria boasts a Balance of Trade value of -2,600, comparable to the US and the UK values as of 2016.

Balance of Payments

Petoria only has a small deficit in concerns of the Balance of Payments. We hold a 2.6 million (Euro) deficit as of Q3 of 2016.

Free Trade?

Our country voted in 2014 against the policy of free trade, which is the belief of eliminating trade barriers between countries.

Currency

Petoria utilizes the South African RAND as its currency, as of 2001
